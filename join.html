ShoreList LLC — Operator Listing Agreement
Plain English · Fair Terms

1. Platform Services

ShoreList LLC agrees to list your business on Shorelist.net, market your experiences to visitors through our website and physical promotional materials, process guest booking deposits on your behalf, notify you of all new bookings by email, and remit your net payout within 5 business days of a completed experience.

2. Your Obligations as an Operator

  • Respond to booking notifications within 24 hours.
  • Maintain all required licenses, permits, and certifications to legally operate your business.
  • Carry appropriate general liability insurance for your activity type.
  • Provide accurate, honest descriptions of your experiences including pricing, duration, and any safety requirements.
  • Deliver confirmed bookings as described.
  • Notify ShoreList within 48 hours of any pricing or availability changes.
  • Treat all ShoreList-referred guests professionally and courteously.
  • Not solicit ShoreList-referred guests to bypass our platform during this agreement and for 12 months after termination.

3. Commission & Payment

ShoreList earns a commission on every completed booking. The commission rate applicable to your category is confirmed in your signed listing agreement. There are no listing fees, monthly fees, or setup charges. ShoreList earns only when you earn. Payouts are processed within 5 business days of the completed experience.

4. Refunds & Cancellations

If you cancel: Guests receive a full refund. You receive no payment for cancelled bookings.

If a guest cancels: ShoreList's standard cancellation policy applies. You are paid only for delivered experiences.

No-shows: You retain the booking deposit for confirmed no-shows.

Disputes: ShoreList will notify you of any guest dispute and give you a reasonable opportunity to respond before any refund decision.

5. Representations & Warranties

By signing, you confirm that you have legal authority to enter this agreement, all information you provide is accurate and complete, your experiences comply with all applicable North Carolina and federal laws, and you will maintain all required operating licenses.

6. Intellectual Property

You grant ShoreList a limited, non-exclusive license to use your business name, photos, and descriptions solely to promote your listings on our platform. You retain full ownership of all your content.

7. Liability & Indemnification

ShoreList is a booking marketplace — we facilitate reservations but do not operate or supervise experiences. ShoreList is not responsible for events occurring during the delivery of your experience. You agree to indemnify ShoreList LLC against third-party claims arising from your business operations. ShoreList's total liability to you is limited to 3 months of commissions paid in the preceding period.

8. Term & Termination

This agreement runs month-to-month from the date of signing. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. ShoreList may terminate immediately for cause including: violation of any applicable law, posing a safety risk to guests, or repeated failure to honor confirmed bookings.

9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep non-public business information — including booking data, guest personal information, and negotiated commission rates — strictly confidential during and after the term of this agreement.

10. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina. You and ShoreList are independent contractors — not partners, employees, or agents of each other. Any unresolved disputes are subject to good-faith mediation in New Hanover County, NC before litigation.

ShoreList LLC recommends that all parties consult qualified legal counsel before signing any business agreement.
