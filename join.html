ShoreList LLC — Operator Listing Agreement Scroll to Read

1. Platform Services

ShoreList LLC agrees to list your business on Shorelist.net, market your experiences to visitors through our website and physical promotional materials, forward booking inquiries to you by email, process guest payments on your behalf, and remit your net payout within 5 business days of a completed experience.

2. Your Obligations as an Operator

  • Respond to booking inquiries within 24 hours.
  • Maintain all required licenses, permits, and certifications to legally operate your business.
  • Carry general liability insurance of at least $1,000,000 per occurrence.
  • Provide accurate, honest descriptions of your experiences including pricing, duration, and any safety requirements.
  • Deliver confirmed bookings as described.
  • Notify ShoreList within 48 hours of any pricing or availability changes.
  • Treat all ShoreList-referred guests professionally.
  • Not solicit ShoreList-referred guests to bypass our platform during this agreement and for 12 months after termination.

3. Commission & Payment

ShoreList earns a commission on every completed booking. The commission rate applicable to your category is shown on your signed agreement. There are no listing fees, monthly fees, or setup charges. ShoreList earns only when you earn. Payouts are made within 5 business days of the completed experience.

4. Refunds & Cancellations

If you cancel: Guests receive a full refund. You receive no payment for cancelled bookings.

If a guest cancels: ShoreList's standard cancellation policy applies. You are paid only for delivered experiences.

No-shows: You keep the booking deposit for confirmed no-shows.

Disputes: ShoreList will notify you of any guest dispute and give you a reasonable opportunity to respond before any refund decision.

5. Representations & Warranties

By signing, you confirm that you have legal authority to enter this agreement, all information you provide is accurate, you hold all required licenses and insurance, and your experiences comply with all applicable laws.

6. Intellectual Property

You grant ShoreList a license to use your business name, photos, and descriptions to promote your listings. You retain full ownership of all your content. You may identify yourself as a "ShoreList Partner" in your own marketing.

7. Liability & Indemnification

ShoreList is a booking marketplace — we facilitate reservations but do not operate or supervise experiences. ShoreList is not responsible for events occurring during the delivery of your experience. You agree to indemnify ShoreList against claims arising from your business operations. ShoreList's total liability to you is capped at 3 months of commissions paid.

8. Term & Termination

This agreement runs month-to-month from the date of signing. Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. ShoreList may terminate immediately if you violate any law, pose a safety risk, or repeatedly fail to honor confirmed bookings.

9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep non-public business information — including booking data, guest information, and commission rates — confidential.

10. General

You and ShoreList are independent contractors — not partners, employees, or agents of each other. This agreement is governed by North Carolina law. Any unresolved disputes are subject to mediation in New Hanover County, NC before litigation.

This summary agreement is intended to be readable and fair. ShoreList LLC recommends that all parties consult qualified legal counsel before signing any business agreement.
